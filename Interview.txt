System Design Concepts Used
1. Layered Architecture

Separated:

controller
service
repository

Interview answer:

“I separated request handling, business logic, and data access into layers for maintainability.”

2. Event-Driven Architecture

Booking emits:

eventBus.emit("APPOINTMENT_BOOKED", appointment);

Listener handles notification.


Interview answer:

“I used event-driven architecture to decouple booking logic from notification logic.”

3. Caching

Available slots are cached:

cache.get("slots")

Interview answer:

“I cached frequently accessed appointment slots to improve response time.”




Interview Q&A
Q1: Why use event-driven here?

Answer:

“Booking an appointment and sending a notification are separate concerns, so event-driven architecture keeps them loosely coupled.”

Q2: Why cache slots?

Answer:

“Slots are read frequently but updated less often, so caching reduces repeated reads.”

Q3: How can this scale?

Answer:

“The notification listener can later be moved to RabbitMQ and cache can be moved to Redis.”
